About Us

We use chess to grow strategic thinking, patience, decision-making, and sportsmanship.

We are a community of students who meet weekly to practice, analyze games, learn openings, solve tactics, and compete in friendly and formal tournaments.

Our sessions are structured so new players can learn confidently while stronger players get serious practice, coaching, and competition preparation.

Weekly practice

Casual games, puzzle races, opening drills, endgame studies, and match reviews.

Mentorship

Experienced players help beginners improve without pressure or intimidation.
